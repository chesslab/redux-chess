What does it mean that ChesslaBlab is a cookie-free website?

chesslablab.com allows to learn and play chess online without being tracked. It does not use cookies of any sort and does not store information as other apps do, not even nicknames or chess games.

Besides, ChesslaBlab is open-source. It can be used by developers to create amazing chess web apps. A bunch of public repos ranging from React and JavaScript to PHP are available on the ChesslaBlab community on GitHub.

What are the chess variants available for playing?

  • Fischer Random 960

What are heuristics in chess?

If you ask a chess pro why a chess move is good, they'll probably give you a bunch of reasons, many of them intuitive, about why they made that decision.

Heuristics are quick, mental shortcuts that we humans use to make decisions and solve problems in our daily lives. While far from being perfect, heuristics are helpful approximations that help the brain save energy and manage cognitive load.
